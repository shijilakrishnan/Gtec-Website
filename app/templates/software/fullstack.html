Full Stack Development

A Full-Stack Developer is someone who is able to work on both the front-end and back-end portions of a website or an application. Front-end generally refers to the portion of a website/application the user will see or interact with, and the back-end is the part of the application that handles the logic,database,interactions,user authentication,server configuration etc.
